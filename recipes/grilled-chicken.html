Grilled Chicken

Recipe for Juicy Grilled Chicken

This grilled chicken is recipe is both delicious and healthy!


Ingredients

Juicy Grilled Chicken

  • 1⁄2 cup lemon juice
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • ground black pepper
  • salt

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat, and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Dip chicken in lemon juice, and sprinkle with onion powder, ground black pepper, seasoning salt, and parsley. Discard any remaining lemon juice.
  3. Grill marinated chicken until no longer pink and juices run clear, about 8 to 10 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
